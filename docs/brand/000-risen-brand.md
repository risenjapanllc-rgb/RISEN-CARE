# Category

**An Observation Intelligence Platform**

RISEN is an Observation Intelligence Platform that transforms observations into understanding and supports better care through explainable artificial intelligence.

---

# Mission

**From Observation to Understanding**

Observation becomes Knowledge.

Knowledge becomes Understanding.

Understanding enables Better Care.

---

# Vision

**Understanding enables Better Care.**

RISEN helps people understand meaningful change before it becomes a crisis.

---

# Guiding Principle

**Observe.**

Collect reliable observations.

**Understand.**

Transform observations into knowledge and understanding.

**Care.**

Support people through timely, explainable, and human-centered care.

---

# AI Philosophy

**AI discovers patterns.**

Artificial Intelligence identifies recurring patterns hidden within large numbers of observations.

**People create meaning.**

Care professionals interpret those patterns within the context of each person's life, values, and environment.

**Together they enable Better Care.**

Human expertise and Artificial Intelligence complement one another to support earlier understanding and better decisions.
