# 1. Purpose

This document defines the Preventive Care Engine of RISEN.

The purpose of the Preventive Care Engine is to transform Observation Intelligence into practical support for care professionals.

RISEN does not exist to predict behavior.

RISEN exists to support better care.

---

# 2. Position within RISEN

The Preventive Care Engine is the final layer of RISEN.

```text
Observation

↓

Knowledge

↓

State

↓

↓

Pattern

↓

Trajectory

↓

Reasoning

↓

Preventive Care
```

Understanding becomes action.

---

# 3. Philosophy

RISEN never recommends care without evidence.

Every recommendation originates from Observation Intelligence.

```text
Observation

↓

Understanding

↓

Care
```

Care is always grounded in observations.

---

# 4. Preventive Care

Preventive Care means supporting professionals before problems become crises.

Examples include

- monitoring
- environmental adjustment
- hydration support
- communication changes
- activity planning
- family collaboration
- medical consultation

Recommendations are support, not instructions.

---

# 5. Care Recommendation

Recommendations are generated from

- current observations
- user state
- repeated patterns
- similar episodes
- historical trajectories

AI integrates these sources into understandable suggestions.

---

# 6. Recommendation Structure

Every recommendation contains

## Observation

Supporting observations.

---

## Evidence

Supporting Knowledge, Episodes, and Patterns.

---

## Reasoning

Why the recommendation was generated.

---

## Suggested Care

Possible actions.

---

## Confidence

Estimated confidence level.

---

Professionals decide.

RISEN supports.

```text
Observation

↓

RISEN
```

# 9. Feedback Loop

Every intervention becomes a new observation.

```text
Observation

↓

Recommendation

↓

Intervention

↓

Outcome

↓

New Observation
```

RISEN continuously learns from care.

---

# 10. Outcome Evaluation

RISEN evaluates whether interventions changed the trajectory.

Possible outcomes

- improved
- unchanged
- deteriorated
- unknown

Outcome evaluation strengthens future reasoning.

---

# 12. Explainability

Every recommendation must answer

Why?

What observations support it?

Which Episodes were similar?

Which Patterns were detected?

What confidence exists?

Nothing should become a black box.

---

# 13. Design Principles

The Preventive Care Engine follows these principles.

1. Observation before recommendation.
2. Evidence before reasoning.
3. AI supports professionals.
4. Recommendations remain explainable.
5. Care remains human-centered.
6. Learning never stops.

---

# 14. Relationship with AI

AI does not decide care.

AI helps organize evidence.

AI explains possible options.

Professionals determine the appropriate intervention.

---

# 15. Final Goal

RISEN is not designed to replace professional expertise.

RISEN helps professionals notice earlier,
understand deeper,
and act with greater confidence.

The ultimate goal is not prediction.

The ultimate goal is better care.

---
