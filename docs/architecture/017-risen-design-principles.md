# 017 RISEN Design Principles

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Last Updated:** 2026-07-09

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the fundamental design principles of RISEN.

Every architecture, algorithm, AI function, and user interface should follow these principles.

---

# 2. Observe Before Judging

Observation comes first.

RISEN always starts with facts.

No interpretation should replace Observation.

```text
Observe

↓

Understand

↓

Care
```

---

# 3. Structure Before AI

AI should never receive raw data if structure can be created first.

RISEN transforms data into structured Observation Intelligence.

```text
Observation

↓

Knowledge

↓

State

↓

Episode

↓

Pattern

↓

AI
```

Structure improves explainability.

---

# 4. AI Supports People

AI supports professional judgment.

AI does not replace professional judgment.

Professionals remain responsible for care decisions.

---

# 5. Explain Everything

Every recommendation should be traceable.

```text
Recommendation

↓

Reasoning

↓

Pattern

↓

Episode

↓

Observation
```

Nothing should become a black box.

---

# 6. Pattern Before Prediction

Prediction is not the goal.

Understanding recurring patterns comes first.

Prediction emerges from understanding.

---

# 7. Probability Over Certainty

RISEN expresses likelihood.

Never certainty.

Use

• may indicate

• could suggest

• should be monitored

Avoid

• caused by

• definitely means

• proves

---

# 8. Episodes Matter

Individual observations are important.

Episodes provide context.

Context creates understanding.

---

# 9. Human Meaning

AI discovers patterns.

People create meaning.

Care always remains human.

---

# 10. Continuous Learning

RISEN improves through accumulated observations.

Every new observation may improve understanding.

The platform continuously evolves.

---

# 11. Platform Vision

RISEN is not an Electronic Health Record.

RISEN is not only an AI system.

RISEN is an Observation Intelligence Platform.

Its purpose is

From Observation

↓

To Understanding

↓

Better Care.

---

# Final Principle

Observe.

Understand.

Care.
